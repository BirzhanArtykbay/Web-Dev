Recipe #1: Kazy(Traditional Kazakh food)

  • Time: 2-3 hours
  • Serves: 6-8
Ingredients Quantity
Horse meat 2 kg
Salt To taste
Natural casing 1 piece
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
Black pepper To taste

How to cook?

  1. Clean the casing thoroughly and set aside.
  2. Slice the meat into long strips and season with salt, pepper, and garlic.
  3. Stuff the casing with the seasoned meat, ensuring it is packed tightly.
  4. Seal the ends of the casing securely.
  5. Boil the kazy in salted water for 2-3 hours until fully cooked.
  6. Slice and serve warm or cold.

Recipe #1: Rib eye Steak

  • Time: 20-30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Beef 0,4 kg
Salt To taste
Onion (sliced) 1 piece
Vegetable oil 2 tbsp
Black pepper To taste

How to cook?

  1. Season the steak cuts with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Sear the steaks for 3-4 minutes on each side until browned.
  4. Add sliced onions to the skillet and sauté alongside the steaks.
  5. Reduce heat and cook to your desired doneness.
  6. Serve the steaks with the sautéed onions on top.
